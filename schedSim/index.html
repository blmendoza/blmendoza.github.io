A game to waste time about not having enough time.

'Schedule Simulator' was created for the 'Embodied Data and Gamified Interfaces' course I took Winter Quarter 2020. I was heavily inspired by my obsession of recording appointments/classes on Google calendar combined with my frantic panic at how easily I con myself into over-filling my schedule.

Disclaimer: it's still in the works! I recommend playing this on a computer!
